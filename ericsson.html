Ericsson & Semcon

User experience design for remote collaboration

Client

Malva Gård

Date

September 2015 - February 2016

Tools

Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, contextual inquiry, anthropological studies, rapid prototyping, personas, mock-ups, digital prototyping

The project concerned how to improve the remote collaboration context and the experience of a remote meeting at the telecom company Ericsson and the consultancy firm Semcon. The aim was to aid users to collaborate more, as the climate in this large company environment combined with technical equipment and available digital tools were hindering the more creative and spontanious environments on remote. The dynamics between participants, some being more dominant and some being more passive, were found to widely affect the outcome and quality of the meeting. We questioned oureselves: how can a user feel inclued and "there" when being thousands of miles away from the person(s) they are having a conversation with? How could we include the users more in the digital meeting room?
