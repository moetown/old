Font-awesome icons
By Moa Parsland 21 June 2017, Wednesday

I figured out how to use icons on the site with Font-awesome, which is basically a piece of code that allows you to use icons on your website without having them downloaded locally. To change a icon, you just simply change a small piece of code instead of going through the process of having to download the icon, choose colors etc. So smooth!
